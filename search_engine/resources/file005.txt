A unique worker drone has woken up from her capsule after many years after every life on Copper 9 went extinct. However, she doesn't remember who she was and will have to find a way to recollect her memory; however, as she goes on a journey, she encounters deadly drones that are out to kill not only her but all the drones. Will she be able to save her many drones by finding who she truly is and what she was made for?
